### Full Description (4000 chars max)

Veill is a privacy-first, end-to-end encrypted messenger built for people who believe their conversations should remain their own.

**Why Veill?**

In a world of data collection and surveillance, Veill puts you back in control. Every message, call, and file you share is encrypted with military-grade protection before it ever leaves your device. Not even we can read your conversations.

**End-to-End Encryption You Can Trust**

Veill uses AES-256-GCM for message encryption and Ed25519 for digital signatures — the same cryptographic standards used by governments and security professionals worldwide. Your messages are encrypted on your device and can only be decrypted by the intended recipient. There are no backdoors, no exceptions.

**Features Built for Privacy**

- **Secure Messaging**: Send text, photos, videos, and files with full end-to-end encryption. Messages are encrypted before they leave your device and can only be decrypted by the recipient.

- **Group Messaging**: Create encrypted group chats with friends, family, or teams. Every member's messages are individually encrypted, ensuring complete privacy for all participants.

- **Voice & Video Calls**: Make crystal-clear encrypted voice and video calls. Your conversations stay private, whether you're talking to one person or a group.

- **Disappearing Messages**: Set messages to auto-delete after a set time. Once they're gone, they're gone — no traces left behind.

- **Offline Mode**: Compose and queue messages even without an internet connection. Veill will securely deliver them when you're back online.

- **Open Source**: Veill is built on open-source technology licensed under MIT. Our encryption protocols are publicly auditable, so you can verify our privacy claims yourself.

**Privacy First, Always**

- Zero data collection — we don't collect, store, or sell your personal information
- No ads, no tracking, no analytics on your conversations
- Metadata minimization — we minimize what we know about your communication patterns
- Full transparency — our code is open source and publicly auditable

**Built for Everyone**

Whether you're a journalist protecting sources, a business safeguarding trade secrets, or simply someone who values privacy, Veill gives you the tools to communicate securely without compromise.

Veill works across Android, iOS, and web, so you can stay connected securely no matter what device you're using.

**Technical Specifications**

- AES-256-GCM encryption for message content
- Ed25519 digital signatures for message authentication
- Perfect forward secrecy for session key exchange
- Zero-knowledge architecture
- Open-source MIT licensed codebase

Download Veill today and take back control of your conversations.
